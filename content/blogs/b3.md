The Robotics Bootcamp 2024 was a massive success, witnessing participation from over 50 students across different faculties.

Day 1 focused on Arduino basics, while Day 2 and 3 involved hands-on assembly and coding. The final competition was intense, with the winning team completing the track in record time.
