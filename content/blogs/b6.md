Nepal's technological landscape is shifting rapidly. With the rise of AI startups and increased interest in machine learning research at universities like Pulchowk Campus, the nation is poised for digital transformation.

### The Academic Shift

Students are no longer just consumers of technology but creators. Recent projects in Natural Language Processing for Nepali language demonstrate this capability.

### Industry Adoption

From banking chat-bots to agricultural prediction models, local industries are beginning to leverage AI to solve unique regional challenges.
